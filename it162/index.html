I've been a Windows user my whole life, but I recently bought a macbook pro. I used to buy gaming laptops, but figured if I was going to be learning design and coding, I wanted something that was going to last for years to come and the m1 max just made sense to me. Overkill for my needs? probably. Worth it? definitely.
