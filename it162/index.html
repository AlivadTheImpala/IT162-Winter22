About me!

Im currently going to school pursuing a Web Design degree. My plan is to help my dad redesign his dog sanctuary web site Green Dog Oasis. I also plan to use my skills to help local businesses in designing a new website if theyre are in need to help get my work out in the real world.

When I'm not working on school I like to decompress by playing video games and delving into a story. RPG's used to be my jam growing up, and while I still love them, I find myself with limited time to actually finish them. Now I try to stick with only quests that interest me or sticking with a game thats a bit more narrative focused.
